Getting There

The main entrance to Fordham University is located at the southeast corner of Columbus Avenue at 60th Street (113 West 60th Street, New York, NY 10023). The A, B, C, D and 1 subway trains all stop at 59th Street/Columbus Circle. The campus is located one block west.

Talks will be located at Fordham Law School, Room 3-04:

    From Columbus Circle, head to Fordham University. Walk two blocks up from 60th St and make a left. Enter the law school building at 150 West 62nd Street. Take the elevator up to the 3rd floor - on the glass side of the building, walk left and down to room 3-04 (note that there are two entrances to each room).

Questions?
